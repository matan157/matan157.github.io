About me

Hello, I'm Matan Uchen. I'm a CS major attending CUNY Brooklyn College. In my free time I design and develop websites for friends, family, or you. As a sophomore at Brooklyn College, I decided to take Intro to C++ with no programming/coding experience. That's when I found my passion. A year after I began programming, I have taught myself: HTML, CSS, Javascript, jQuery, and Bootstrap 3—all in my free time. At this point I have three functional projects. I plan to continue creating, learning, and growing.

Apart from developing, I am a musician. Whether it's on trombone or guitar, I have played thirteen different broadway shows with several different theatres. Some of these include Fiddler on the Roof, Les Miserables, Hairspray, and Crazy for You. When I'm not in the pit, I'm on the stage playing with my band Eli Whitney and the Sound Machine.
